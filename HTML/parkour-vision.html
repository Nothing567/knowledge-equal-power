Parkour Vision

=what is & how to get it=

Let's say that you already started parkour, but you don't find
spot for that. What if I will say that you already have places to practice and you just don't
see them? Here I will give you a few things that you can use in parkour and you can find them
almost anywhere + recomended places where you can practice safely by yourself.

What is it?

Parkour Vision is the ability to imaginate and to see obstacles almost everywhere. Let's take for example a curb. What can you do with a curb? At first maybe you'll say nothing, but if you think more you can already see that you can do may many things on a curb.
