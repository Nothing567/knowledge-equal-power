How to overcome your fears

=presented step by step=

All of us have fears, but how we choose to manage them represent a little big part of us.
Here I will present you some good ways to overcome fears, even if they are little or big.

The first step will be always is to identify the fear you want to overcome. Then to identify the type of fear and choose the right cope mechanism for it. Here are some of them.

1.When you need to do an action right into the moment, but you fear to say or do it

When you have situations when you need to say something or do something now and not later, this strategy
is a good cope mechanism with these type of fears. All what you need to do is this:

1.To countdown 3 seconds or more, but no more than 5 seconds.
2.And then to say or make the action you fear, but you need also to do it.
