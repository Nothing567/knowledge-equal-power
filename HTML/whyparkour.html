Why to learn parkour?

=the 10 benefits of parkour=

You still ask yourself if to learn parkour or not?Or you ask youself why some people choose to learn parkour instead of tennis or something else more simple and less dangerous?Here are some reasons why you or other may doing parkour.


1.You overcome your fears.

Maybe without realizing, in learning new parkour moves you overcome your fears step by step, especially if you learn parkour by yourself. Just be sure that your are doing it safely.

If you want to read about how to overcome your fears click here.







2.You exercise

Parkour is a sport and like any other sport you exercise and this is very good for your own physical and mental health.


3.You learn how to control your body

Parkour is also about controling your body. You cannot do parkour without learning how to control your body. It comes in the package of learning parkour.


4.You can escape from dangerous things

Are you chased by killers, kidnapers, bullies or even by an agresive dog? No problem. You can use parkour to escape from them.


5.You can escape from the buildings that are in fire

Now with parkour you can do many things. Especially escape from any kind fo buidings(even the buildings that are in fire).


6.You can be the hero of the game

The ball that you and your friends you were you using to play with is now blocked somewhere where you cannot pick it? No problem, now you can use parkour to go and get that ball.


7.You can save lives of other people

If someone else is in danger you can save them.


8.You can feel as a hero when you do parkour

Just knowing that you can move like heroes make you feel as a hero.


9.You don't need special clothes

You can do parkour in any clothes you want. It don't require any special costume.

You can practice parkour everywhere

You can also practice parkour on the streets, at schools, in shops, etc. For short, you can do parkour everywhere.


10.You can impress other people with your parkour moves

Once you are a pro at parkour you can impress other people with your parkour moves.
